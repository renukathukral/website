About

Renuka is an educationist with an experience of over 25 years in teaching, administration, and teacher training. She has led and conducted multiple conferences on the benefits and applications of experiential learning in a progressive schooling environment for school principals and directors across PAN India. In addition, she has presented sessions on engaging and innovating teaching methods in over 60 prominent schools across India for teachers teaching pre-primary and primary classes.

At present, she is the Academic Head at KPL Learning where she works towards conceptualizing novel learning aids to enhance cognitive thinking and fine motor skills of students at the primary level. Renuka is a passionate teacher with a motivation to propagate ideas of progressive education and nurturing young minds. Besides her academic interests, she also fulfils her roles as the Director of her tech consultancy, Isri Solutions Pvt. Ltd. She is a certified NLP Counselling Coach and Certified Auditor for ISO 21001:2018 (Management System for Education System). Renuka is a strong believer in the values of empathy, respect towards society and environment and is sure that every day brings something new to learn.
