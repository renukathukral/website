About

Renuka is an educationist with an experience of over 25 years in teaching, administration, and teacher training. She has led and conducted multiple conferences on the benefits and applications of experiential learning in a progressive schooling environment for school principals and directors across PAN India. In addition, she has presented sessions on engaging and innovating teaching methods in over 60 prominent schools across India for teachers teaching pre-primary and primary classes.
